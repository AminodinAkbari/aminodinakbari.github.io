I'm a 25-year-old Backend Engineer with more than three years of hands-on experience building web applications and backend services.

My primary focus is Python and FastAPI. While I don't consider myself a specialist in every aspect of the ecosystem, I've spent the last several years working with these technologies in real-world environments and production systems.

Along the way, I've gained practical experience with Linux servers, Docker, deployment workflows, and fundamental security concepts. Most of my learning comes directly from official documentation, hands-on experimentation, and solving real engineering problems rather than relying solely on tutorials.

I regularly use AI tools as learning assistants, code reviewers, and productivity aids, but I prefer understanding the systems I work with rather than treating generated code as a black box. If I cannot explain how something works, I do not consider it production-ready.

Previously, I worked remotely with a German startup, where I contributed to backend development, maintenance, testing, deployments, and infrastructure-related tasks. Unfortunately, the company ceased operations due to the long-term impact of internet disruptions affecting collaboration with developers in Iran.

Currently, I am working on a fintech price-monitoring platform while continuing to improve my skills in software architecture, distributed systems, and frontend development with React. I'm also developing my personal blog using Django and Django REST Framework.

I enjoy being part of a team with long-term goals and believe meaningful software is built through collaboration, ownership, and continuous learning.
